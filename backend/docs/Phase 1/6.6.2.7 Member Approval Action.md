# Approval action
`PATCH /users/admin/{id}/{requestAction}`
Remember to include bearer token of admin
requestAction have 3 types:
`approve`, `reject` & `request_info`
- when `approve` is sent, `status` of company, location, document & location-document changed to `active`
- when `reject` & `reject_reason` are sent, `status` of company, location, document & location-document changed to `rejected`
- when `request_info` & `message` are sent, `status` of company, location, document & location-document changed to `request_information`
- Out of three action will return error
## cURL of request_info action
```bash
curl -L -X PATCH 'localhost:3000/users/admin/5/request_info' \
-H 'Authorization: Bearer eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJpZCI6MTAsImdsb2JhbFJvbGUiOiJhZG1pbiIsImlzSGF1bGllciI6ZmFsc2UsImNyZWF0ZWRBdCI6IjIwMjUtMDYtMTNUMTM6MDI6MzAuNTg1WiIsImNvbXBhbnlJZCI6MCwiaWF0IjoxNzQ5ODE5NzUwLCJleHAiOjM0OTk2NDk1MDB9.s6bJDowB6leQ9wgc2nAqb_IwgvOsDldPuCieaKgqB9I' \
```
